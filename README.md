# Physiad
The LaTeX source code for the book *Tuyển tập các bài tập điện từ trong các kỳ thi Olympic (2021)*  
*(English: Selected Problems in Electromagnetism from Physics Olympiads)*

This book is intended for high school students preparing for National, Regional, or International Physics Olympiads. It is also suitable for early undergraduate students interested in competitions such as Undergraduate Physics Olympiads (i.e., *Olympic Vật lý sinh viên*).

The problems are carefully collected from various physics competitions and cover a range of topics in electromagnetism. Some may require familiarity with advanced mathematical concepts typically introduced in the first or second year of college, including derivatives, integrals, and vector calculus, etc.
